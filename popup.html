Common Regex Patterns:

  • ^https?://localhost:\\d+/.* - Any localhost with port
  • ^https?://.*\\.example\\.com/.* - Any subdomain of example.com
  • ^https?://.*\\.zoom\\.us/j/\\d+.*#success$ - Zoom success pages
Note: Use \\ to escape special characters like dots
